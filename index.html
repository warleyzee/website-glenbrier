About Us

Glenbrier LTD is a private limited company, registered to Dunmoe Lodge, Navan, Co Meath, Ireland. The company was formed in Navan in 1996 after 25 years of successful trading in the UK. The company is built on a foundation of achievement within the concrete frame sector both in Ireland and the UK. We are established as a leading developer in the residential market completing various developments of detached, semi detached and apartment developments. A number of our commercial projects have become flagship operations for our clients.
